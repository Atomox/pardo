Featured Investments

Cherner Properties

Cherner Properties owns and operates varied commercial real estate interests throughout the Mid-Atlantic U.S.

The View at Tysons

Cherner sold +/- 7.5 acres of land in Vienna, VA, capitalizing on a lengthy re-zoning and entitlement process. The site is a planned development project that will comprise of 3MM+ SF of mixed-use space.

The Cosmopolitan at Reston Town Center

Cosmopolitan is a 2007-built, 289-unit multifamily property located in Reston, VA.

The Lofts at Hillson

Lofts at Hillson is a 2020-built, 70-unit multifamily property located in Nashville, TN.

The Eastland

Eastland is a 2017-built, 49-unit multifamily property with 3,500 SF of retail space located in Nashville, TN.

511 Queens

511 Queens is a 2018-built, 64-unit multifamily property located in Charlotte, NC.

Lake Pleasant Pavilion

Lake Pleasant Pavilion is a 178,403 RSF power retail shopping center located in Peoria, Arizona. Cherner is the mezzanine lender on the property.

Walnut Commons

Walnut Commons is a 2013-built, 100-unit multifamily property located in Chattanooga, TN.

Bread Factory Lofts

Bread Factory Lofts is a 1920s-built bread factory converted into a 26-unit multifamily property in 2003, located in Chattanooga, TN.

Artemis Midtown

Artemis is a 2015-built, 146-unit multifamily property located in Nashville, TN.

Farmhouse Hillson

Hillson is a 2021-built, 248-unit multifamily property located in Nashville, TN.

Deerbrook Marketplace

Deerbrook Marketplace is a 360,250 RSF power retail shopping center located in Humble, Texas. Cherner is the mezzanine lender on the property.

Ellis at Germantown

Ellis is a 2013-built, 92-unit multifamily property located in Nashville, TN.

1577 Spring Hill Road

1577 is a 121,000 RSF office building with 7,100 SF of retail space located in Vienna, VA.

The View at Muriel

The View at Muriel is a 2021-built boutique townhome project located in Pittsburgh, PA.

Caliber Collision

Caliber Collision is the largest collision repair company in the United States. Cherner is Caliber’s Development Partner throughout the Mid-Atlantic. Cherner sold its first Caliber portfolio to Realty Income (NYSE:O) in 2021 and is growing its second portfolio.

Go! Wash & Waves Car Wash

Cherner develops, owns and operates a portfolio of car washes in the Mid-Atlantic under the brand names Go! Wash and Waves.

Capital Automotive REIT

Cherner is a founding member of Capital Automotive REIT (CARS). In 1997, Cherner joined three car dealer families to take the real estate investment trust public. Brookfield Properties acquired CARS in 2014.

Rapid Finance

Cherner founded RapidAdvance (now Rapid Finance) and sold the company to Rockbridge Growth Equity in 2013. Rapid Finance is a technology-powered financial services company that provides working capital to small and medium-sized businesses in the United States. Cherner is now a senior, secured lender to the company.

Elevate Funding

Cherner founded Elevate Funding, a small business funding company based out of Gainesville, FL. Elevate’s mission is to help merchants get back on their feet, catch up with expenses, and continue to run successful businesses.

Holistic Industries

Cherner owns a minority stake in Holistic Industries, the largest private vertically integrated multi- state operator in the cannabis industry.

Full Circle Capital Management

Cherner is a partner to Full Circle Capital Management, a private credit platform dedicated exclusively to the hospitality industry.
