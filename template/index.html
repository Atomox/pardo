Who We Are

Founded in 1930, Cherner Capital (previously Cherner Automotive Group & Cherner Development Group) is a multi-generational real estate-focused investment firm & family office based in Washington, D.C. and New York City.

Long Term Value Creation

Cherner places equity and debt investments in real estate and non-real estate assets and businesses to optimize value and investment returns across the risk-reward spectrum. The company analyzes investment opportunities with a long-term time horizon to execute on its strategy of creating lasting value for generations.
